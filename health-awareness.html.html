Disease Awareness

Cancer

Early detection is crucial. Avoid smoking, maintain a healthy diet, and get regular screenings.

Diabetes

Monitor sugar intake, exercise regularly, and maintain a healthy weight to prevent type 2 diabetes.

Heart Disease

Maintain heart health with regular exercise, a balanced diet, and controlling stress and cholesterol.

Mental Health

Practice mindfulness, get adequate sleep, and seek support for stress or anxiety.

Hypertension

Reduce salt intake, exercise regularly, and monitor blood pressure frequently.

For awareness only. Consult healthcare professionals for diagnosis and treatment.
